Events Leading to the Quiet Revolution

1. The Quiet Revolution Begins

Why it matters: In 1960, the Liberal Party led by Jean Lesage came to power in Quebec. They wanted to make big changes to Quebec schools, healthcare, and the economy. This is when the Quiet Revolution started, a time when many changes in Quebec took place.

2. Rise of Quebec Nationalism

Why it matters:Before the Quiet Revolution, lots of people in Quebec started to feel that they should have more control over their own things. They wanted Quebec to be its own nation and seperate from the rest of Canada. This idea became stronger during this time.

3. The Duplessis Era

Why it matters: Maurice Duplessis was the leader of Quebec from 1944 to 1959. He kept things normal and didn't want big changes. Many people in Quebec were unhappy with how things were being ran by him, which made them want change even more when Jean Lesage took over.
